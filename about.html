About Us

We Care

RecoverMe is an organization dedicated to helping drug/alcohol addicted individuals identify and treat the problem/problems that may have contributed to their addiction. We believe that it is greatly important to treat the whole self, not just to stop the drink or the drug. We are here to help connect addicts in recovery with the help, services, and support needed for them to succeed in their recovery and to lead a happy healthy life after addiction. We do this by treating the Mind, Body, and Spirit.
