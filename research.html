Research

Research Shows

Recovery is about so much more than putting down the drink/drug. That is the most important step, but it is only the beginning of the journey. There are many areas that should be addressed. This is where it is imporatnt to look inward and start healing by looking at root causes and past trauma. Sometimes it hurts to heal, but the hurt is only temporary and can open the door to so much happiness!

Overcoming Addiction

It is not always easy maintaining sobriety, but it is always worth the effort! Recovery is often attained with a complete overhaul of lifestyle. For many, this means changing everything, from the food they eat, to learning to sustain healthy relationships. A personal program of recovery should address all aspects of the recovering addicts life.

Stay Strong

Sobriety effects so many people in our lives. Our actions impact more than just ourselves. Addiction effects the whole family and so recovery should also include the whole family. It is important to incorporate all aspects of the self toward healing. The better a person feels in recovery, the more they are likely to stick with it and to succeed long term.
